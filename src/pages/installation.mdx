## For iPhone

Open the App Store application on your iOS device. Once open, search for Nyxo: Sleep coaching and install the application to your iOS device. Now you can open the Nyxo app and start adding your sleep data.

## For Android

Open the Play Store application on your Android device. Once open, search for Nyxo: Sleep coaching and install the application to your Android device. Now you can open the Nyxo app and start adding your sleep data.
