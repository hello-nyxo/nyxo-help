Nyxo Cloud is a safe place for your sleep data and Nyxo Sleep Coaching progress in case you lose your device or change devices. Nyxo Cloud also allows you to see your personal data online and you can access your Nyxo Sleep Coaching. To access Nyxo Cloud, you need to create a Nyxo account
