To purchase a subscription plan for Nyxo Sleep Coaching, go to the Coaching tab and you will see a note saying “Subscribe Now”. Click that note and you will be taken to the available subscription plans. 
 
The current subscription plans are:

+ Monthly
+ Quarterly
+ Annually (best value)
